## Current Product Story

- Music is relation, not only content.
- Rooms are the social core: create a room, share a link, listen together.
- Guests should not need a wallet to feel the room.
- Artists should keep sovereignty over catalog, access, rights, and royalties.
- Web3 should be felt as trust, not bureaucracy.
